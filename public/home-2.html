1. What is a Seed Ball?

Definition

A seed ball is a small, compact mixture of soil, clay, and seeds that is designed to make planting easier and more effective.

Purpose

Seed balls protect seeds from being eaten by birds or insects, and from harsh environmental conditions like drought or intense sunlight. They ensure seeds are deposited with some initial nutrients and moisture, giving them a better chance of sprouting and growing.

Historical Context

The use of seed balls dates back to ancient agricultural practices.
